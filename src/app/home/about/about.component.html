About Aarambh

Aarambh The Creek Breeze Resort is a premium, peaceful getaway located near the pristine Kelve Beach. Known for our luxury villa-like feel at a budget price, we provide the perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Surrounded by calming backwaters and towering coconut trees, our property offers spacious A.C. rooms, a well-maintained swimming pool, and wide open spaces perfect for relaxation, family gatherings, or small intimate events.

Our commitment to heartfelt hospitality ensures every guest is treated like family. Experience genuine care, tranquility, and nature's finest at Aarambh.
