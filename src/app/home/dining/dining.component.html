100% Homemade Meals

What truly sets us apart is the love and care poured into our food. Every meal you eat here is 100% homemade, personally prepared by the mother of our owner, Mr. Milind Gharat. This rare, authentic touch ensures that your food is always fresh, delicious, and deeply comforting—just like home.

Coastal Fresh Catch

Our specialty lies in authentic coastal cuisine. We serve the freshest catch of the day, prepared with traditional local spices. Whether you crave a perfectly fried Surmai or a spicy prawn curry, our non-vegetarian spread guarantees an unforgettable culinary journey.

Farm to Table Vegetarian

Our dedicated vegetarian kitchen sources organic produce directly from local farms. From hearty Maharashtrian delicacies to simple, light meals, we ensure every dish is vibrant and bursting with natural flavors. Enjoy a true farm-to-table experience with every bite.
