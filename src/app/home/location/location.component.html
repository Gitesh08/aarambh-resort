Getting Here

By Train

Take a Western Line local train to Palghar or Kelve Road station. Autos or private taxis are readily available to reach the resort in ~15-20 minutes.

By Road

Enjoy a scenic drive from Mumbai (~2.5 hrs) via NH48. Follow directions towards Manor/Palghar and take the exit to Kelwa Beach. Ample parking space is available.
